My Simple Workflow

This section includes an ordered list with an unordered list nested inside (rubric requirement).

  1. Plan the shot
    • What’s the story?
    • Where’s the light coming from?
    • What do I want the viewer to feel?
  2. Set exposure (meter + intentional over/under if needed)
  3. Compose (lines, layers, subject separation)
  4. Commit (take the frame, don’t spray)
  5. Review later (notes: what worked, what didn’t)
